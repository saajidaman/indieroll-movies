About The IndieRoll

We believe discovering great films should be free, simple, and open to everyone.

Why we publish movie guides for free

Many people love cinema but do not always know where to start. Paid platforms often hide recommendations behind subscriptions, adverts, or complicated accounts. The IndieRoll was created so anyone can browse a curated catalogue, filter by genre, and share honest reviews without paying a fee.

Film discovery should not depend on how much you can spend. By keeping this guide free, we make it easier for students, casual viewers, and movie fans to explore titles, compare ratings, and decide what to watch next.

What you can do here

  • Browse movies with title, genre, release year, and rating details
  • Filter the catalogue by Action, Comedy, Drama, or Science Fiction
  • Submit feedback through our review form
  • Use the site on desktop or mobile with a clear, accessible layout

Our values

Open access

No paywall. The catalogue and review tools are available to every visitor.

Clear guidance

We focus on useful details — genre, year, and rating — so choices feel simple.

Community voice

Your reviews help other people understand which films are worth their time.

Join in

Explore the free catalogue on the Home page, or tell us what you thought of a film on the Write a Review page. Every contribution helps keep movie discovery friendly and open.
